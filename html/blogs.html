CATT New Team 2020-2021

Oct 26, 2020


The Chinese Association Tricking Team (CATT) recently held their virtual team tryouts for potential new and experienced trickers to join. Based at the University of California, Irvine, CATT is one of the only collegiate tricking teams in the world. Since 2017, they developed a growing presence in the local and even international tricking communities. As of last Tuesday Oct. 26th, they released the roster for the newest generation of members. The tricking community is beyond excited to see what they have in store for the coming year, despite the limitations posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.


S . O . B .

Aug 30, 2020


Ethan Turner, one of the most well-rounded trickers in the US, is known for embodying pure perfection in nearly all of his tricks. He shows immense skills in his martial arts kicks, his combo transitions, and especially his acrobatic twists. Yesterday, he uploaded a new montage on his YouTube channel showcasing his training in the recent months. The video, named S.O.B., has taken the tricking world by storm. The video editing and music further enhances his already impressive execution of tricks displayed. Once again, Turner continues to develop his already popular image through his talent, and we hope to see more content from him soon.


Loopkicks Gathering 2020 Cancelled Due to COVID-19

Jun 2, 2020


Loopkicks Gathering is a highly famed tricking gathering known to unite diverse trickers from all over the world to train together and make lifelong connections with each other. Perhaps one of the biggest events of the year, many trickers including myself were once again excited for the the gathering which was scheduled to take place from Jul 16, 2020 Jul 19, 2020. to Jul 19, 2020. However, we are saddened by the annoucement made by Loopkicks that the event is understandably cancelled due to the dangers of the COVID-19 outbreak. This left many attendees to unfortunately reschedule their plans, flights, etc. It is a very hard time for all of us to deal with this pandemic, but health and safety is a priority. Maybe 2021 will be the year that Loopkicks Gathering will return...I hope.
