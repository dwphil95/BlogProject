WELCOME TO TRICK CENTRAL!

For those of you who are unfamiliar, the sport of martial arts tricking is an aesthetic blend of martial arts kicks, gymnastics, acrobatic flips, break dancing, and many other styles of movement. It takes elements from these physical disciplines to create visually pleasing combinations of unimaginable feats performed by the athlete, known as a tricker.

Here at Trick Central, we provide updates on the latest tricking related news through blog articles written by yours truly. Whether you are new to the sport or have been practicing for years, we have all you need here! Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or concerns on our contact form!
